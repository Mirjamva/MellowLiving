Welcome to Your Co-Living Project

Discover a vibrant community where you can connect, grow, and thrive. Our co-living spaces are designed to foster deep connections and authentic expression, creating a nourishing environment for both individuals and the world.

About Us

Founded by a group of 20 young professionals, Your Co-Living Project aims to create a safe, healthy, and caring community in the heart of Berlin. Our residents enjoy a unique living experience that combines private living spaces with shared amenities and a strong sense of belonging.

Our Spaces

Our co-living spaces are designed to be comfortable, functional, and stylish. Each resident has a private bedroom and bathroom, while shared areas include a spacious living room, fully-equipped kitchen, laundry facilities, and outdoor spaces for relaxation and socializing.

Amenities and Services

At Your Co-Living Project, we believe in providing our residents with everything they need to thrive. Our amenities and services include high-speed Wi-Fi, regular cleaning, 24/7 security, community events, and access to coworking spaces and recreational facilities.
